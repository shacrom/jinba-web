### Priority inspection points

- **Sill rails and rocker panels**: critical structural rust areas.
- **Floorpan and wheel arches**: lift carpet and inspect from underneath.
- **Wings**: repair-panel swaps are very common; check continuity.
- **L24 engine**: timing-chain noise and compression test.
- **SU carburettors**: synchronisation, shaft wear.

### Documentation

- **Chassis number check**: cloned or swapped identities are common on imported cars.
