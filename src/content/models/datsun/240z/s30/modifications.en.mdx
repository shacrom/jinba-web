### Engine and fuelling

- Triple Weber DCOE carburettors on a dedicated manifold.
- 6-3-1 header exhaust.

### Brakes

- Four-piston front calipers (common: Wilwood or Toyota 4-pot).

### Suspension

- Polyurethane bushings throughout.
- Progressive springs and gas dampers.
