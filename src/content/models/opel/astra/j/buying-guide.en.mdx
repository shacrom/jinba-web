### Priority inspection points

- **1.4 Turbo timing chain**: plastic guides — cold-start rattle = replace.
- **CDTi EGR**: soot clogging; warning light and power loss.
- **CDTi DPF**: urban use cuts regens; high oil consumption follows.
- **1.4T turbo sensor and wastegate**: overboost / underboost.
- **Interior electronics**: CD30/CD40/Navi 600 units with intermittent faults.

### Target price (EUR, Spanish market)

- Naturally-aspirated 1.4 petrol: **€3,500–5,500**.
- 1.6/1.7 CDTi with history: **€5,000–7,500**.
- OPC (2.0 T 280 hp): **€12,000–18,000** depending on condition.
