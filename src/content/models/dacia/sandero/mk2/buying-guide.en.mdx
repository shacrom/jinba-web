### Priority inspection points

- **0.9 TCe (H4Bt) timing chain**: cold-start noise = guide wear.
- **EDC (dry dual-clutch)**: low-speed jerkiness.
- **1.5 dCi injectors**: common failure — smoke, power loss.
- **LPG bi-fuel system**: inspect tank and valves if the car has run mostly on LPG.
- **Rear suspension**: worn bushes after rural use on poor road surfaces.

### Target price (EUR, Spanish market)

- Basic 1.2 petrol: **€3,000–5,500**.
- 0.9 TCe or 1.5 dCi with history: **€5,500–8,500**.
- Late Stepway with low mileage: **€9,000–12,000**.
