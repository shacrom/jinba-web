The MX-5 NA defined the modern roadster. B6 1.6 engine producing 90 hp, or BP 1.8 with 133 hp from 1994. Fibreglass body, rack-and-pinion steering, and a double-wishbone rear suspension. One of the best value-for-satisfaction ratios in the accessible classics market.
