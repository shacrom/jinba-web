- Chassis: steel monocoque, double-wishbone front and rear.
- Engines: B6 1.6 L (90 hp, 1989–1997) and BP 1.8 L (131–133 hp, from 1994).
- Transmission: 5-speed manual (4-speed auto rare in Europe).
- Rear-wheel drive, manual soft-top, pop-up headlights.
- Total production: ~431,500 units, built in Hiroshima.
