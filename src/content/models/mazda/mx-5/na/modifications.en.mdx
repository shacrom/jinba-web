### Suspension

- Lowering springs (Tein S-Tech, Eibach Pro-Kit): –25 to –35 mm, stock dampers.
- Entry-level coilovers (BC Racing BR, Meister R): adjustable ride height and damping.
- Anti-roll bars: tune understeer/oversteer balance by bar diameter.

### Engine and exhaust

- 2.25″ cat-back exhaust: modest gain, signature sound.
- Short-ram intake with closed airbox: better throttle response; avoid open pod filters.
- Spark plugs and leads: maintenance, not a mod.

### Aesthetics and practical

- OEM or replica hardtop: winter use, adds chassis stiffness.
- 15″ wheels (Panasport, Rota RB): keep original overall diameter.
- Bolt-in roll bar (Hard Dog, etc.): required for track use.
