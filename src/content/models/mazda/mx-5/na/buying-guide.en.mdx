### Priority inspection points

- **Door sills**: lift carpet; look for rust from the inside out.
- **Rear wheel arches and rocker panels**: common corrosion areas.
- **Boot / trunk**: check seals; moisture means soft-top or seal failure.
- **Soft top**: latch, fabric free of tears, clear rear window.
- **Valve-cover gasket (B6)**: frequent oil leak point.
- **Clutch and synchros**: grinding on 2nd and 3rd gears.
- **Rear chassis below dampers**: watch for impact deformation.

### Target price (EUR, Spanish market)

- Usable, no major rust: **€6,000** minimum.
- Well-kept with full history: **€9,000–13,000**.
- Fresh JDM imports (Eunos Roadster): highly variable — verify paperwork.
