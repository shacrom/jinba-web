### Suspension / tyres

- Lighter 14" or 15" wheels to reduce unsprung mass.
- Class-A eco tyres — small gain in urban fuel economy.

### Cosmetic

- Plastic arch and sill trims (hides emerging rust perception).
- Spanish-legal tint: 70% front glass, 20% rear glass.

### Audio

- Aftermarket 2-DIN Android head unit + reversing camera — pre-facelift Picantos had no modern integration.
