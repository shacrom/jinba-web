- First-generation Picanto, A-segment city car.
- 1.0 G4HE 61 hp and 1.1 G4HG 65 hp (both petrol, naturally aspirated).
- 5-speed manual (4-speed automatic optional on some trims).
- Front-wheel drive.
- 2007 facelift (grille, headlights, interior).
- Strong seller in Spain as a second car / first car; cheap to maintain.
- Chassis: SA.
