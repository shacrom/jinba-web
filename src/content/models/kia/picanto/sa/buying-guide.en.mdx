### Priority inspection points

- **Underbody rust**: rear wheel arches and sills — weak spots especially on coastal cars.
- **Clutch**: >150k km clutches are typically end-of-life; noise on pedal press or slip in 5th.
- **Manual gearbox**: worn 2nd-gear synchros common; shift at moderate speed and listen.
- **Air conditioning**: compressor failure on units without preventive service.
- **Shock absorbers**: soft OEM suspension fatigues early — check rebound.
- **Timing belt**: 90-120k km replacement; G4HE/G4HG engines are non-interference but the water pump usually goes with an old belt.

### What to ask the seller

- Full service book with oil changes up to date.
- Recent ITV (Spanish MoT) with no major defects.
- Pre-purchase drive: long run with AC at full, plus low-speed manoeuvres.
