Second-generation A3, Audi's premium compact. Shares the **PQ35** platform with the VW Golf Mk5/Mk6, SEAT León 1P, and Škoda Octavia II. **Facelift 8PA** in 2008 (single-frame grille, revised headlights). The **RS3 8PA** arrived only in 2011 with the 2.5 TFSI inline-five.

### Transmissions

- 5- or 6-speed manual depending on engine.
- **6-speed DSG S-tronic (02E/DQ250)** on 2.0+ petrol and 2.0 diesel.
- **7-speed S-tronic (DQ200)** dry-clutch on 1.4 TFSI and 1.6 TDI CR — known clutch and mechatronics issues.

### Drivetrain

- Front-wheel drive on most variants.
- **quattro** (Haldex Gen 2/3) on S3, RS3, 3.2 V6, and some 2.0 TDI 170.
