### Priority inspection points

- **Intercooler**: condition and mounting, especially on S3.
- **2.0 TFSI oil consumption**: verify level and trend; crankcase breather valve is the usual culprit.
- **Haldex transmission (quattro)**: pump and oil service; focus on S3 models.
- **Coolant temperature sensors**: intermittent failure is a known issue.
