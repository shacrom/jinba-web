- Third generation of the RS6, wagon bodystyle only (Avant).
- 4.0 TFSI V8 biturbo shared with the S8 and RS7.
- Pre-facelift 2013–2015: 560 hp.
- Facelift / Performance 2016–2018: 605 hp.
- 8-speed tiptronic automatic and permanent quattro AWD.
- 0–62 mph: 3.9 s (560 hp) / 3.7 s (Performance).
- Top speed electronically limited to 155 mph (190 mph with Dynamic Plus pack).
- Adaptive air suspension (DRC on Performance).
