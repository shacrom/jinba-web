### Engine

- Stage 1 (tune only): ~680–720 hp.
- Stage 2 with catless downpipes + intakes: 750+ hp.
- Intercooler upgrade (Wagner, CSF): required for sustained hard use with a tune.

### Exhaust

- Cat-back Milltek / Capristo: sound and weight gains.
- X-pipe valves for volume control.

### Suspension / brakes

- Eibach Pro-Kit springs (air-suspension compatible).
- Ferodo DS2500 or Pagid RS29 pads for track use.
- RS7 Performance big-brake kit (better heat dissipation, keeps OEM wheels).

### Drivetrain

- ZF 8HP service every 60k km (Lifeguard 8 fluid).
- Haldex service does NOT apply — the RS6 uses a Torsen centre diff plus DPC rear diff.
