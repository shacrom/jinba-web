### Priority inspection points

- **Timing chains** (2013–2014): check for cold-start rattle; tensioner recall before 2015.
- **DPC (Dynamic Performance Control, active rear diff)**: oil leaks and sensor faults; expensive repair.
- **Air suspension**: compressor and bellows; check lift time and compressor noise.
- **Auxiliary electric water pumps**: two units; common failure around 120–150k km.
- **Intake carbon buildup**: direct injection — inspect intake valves.
- **ZF 8HP automatic**: oil service every 60k km (not lifetime despite Audi's claim).
- **Injectors**: premature wear on bad fuel or aggressive tunes.

### What to ask the seller

- Full service history from Audi or a specialist.
- Recent VCDS / ODIS scan with no active codes.
- Drive cold and hot (leaks and noises often only show warm).
