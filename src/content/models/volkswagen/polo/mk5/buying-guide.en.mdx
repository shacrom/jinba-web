### Priority inspection points

- **1.2 TSI (EA111) timing chain**: cold-start rattle = replace immediately.
- **7-speed dry DSG (DQ200)**: low-speed jerkiness; check oil service history.
- **Electric water pump**: leaks and temperature warnings.
- **Oil level sensor**: frequent false positives.
- **1.4 TSI GTI turbo**: blue smoke on hard acceleration.

### Target price (EUR, Spanish market)

- High-mileage basic diesel: **€4,500–6,500**.
- 1.2 TSI with history: **€6,500–9,000**.
- GTI (1.4 TSI 180 hp): **€10,000–14,000**.
