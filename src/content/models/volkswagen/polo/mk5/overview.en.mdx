- Fifth-generation Polo (6R 2009–2014; 6C facelift 2014–2017).
- VAG PQ25 platform, shared with Ibiza Mk4 and Audi A1 8X.
- Petrol: 1.2 TSI (90 hp), 1.4 TSI (140–192 hp in GTI/Blue GT).
- Diesel: 1.2/1.6 TDI (75–105 hp).
- Front-wheel drive; 5/6-speed manual or 7-speed dry DSG (DQ200).
