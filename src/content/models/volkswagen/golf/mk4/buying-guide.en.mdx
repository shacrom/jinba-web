### Priority inspection points

- **Ignition coil pack (1.8T)**: endemic failure — listen for cold-start stumble and part-throttle misfires.
- **ABS module**: permanent dashboard warning means repair or replacement needed.
- **Boot / trunk**: water ingress through the rear seal; check carpet and spare-wheel well.
- **Electric windows**: plastic regulator guides crack; window drops or sits crooked.
- **V6 R32 coils**: less frequent but six individual coils — check all.
