### Priority inspection points

- **DPF (TDCi)**: clogging on urban-use cars; failed regenerations.
- **Dual-mass flywheel + clutch (TDCi)**: idle vibration, rattle.
- **Throttle position sensor (petrol)**: irregular idle and hesitation.
- **MAF sensor**: power loss during acceleration.
- **Tailgate corrosion**: paint bubbles, especially on sedan and 5-door.

### Target price (EUR, Spanish market)

- High-mileage 1.6 petrol: **€3,000–4,500**.
- 1.6/2.0 TDCi with history: **€4,500–7,000**.
- ST (2.5 T 225 hp): **€8,000–12,000** depending on condition.
