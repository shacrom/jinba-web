- Second-generation Focus, built on the Ford C1 platform (shared with Volvo S40/V50 and Mazda 3 BK).
- Body styles: 3/5-door, sedan, estate, Coupé-Cabriolet.
- Petrol: 1.4/1.6/1.8/2.0 Duratec; 2.5 T Duratec RS (305 hp) and ST (225 hp).
- Diesel: 1.6/1.8/2.0 TDCi (90–136 hp).
- Front-wheel drive; 5/6-speed manual or 4-speed automatic.
