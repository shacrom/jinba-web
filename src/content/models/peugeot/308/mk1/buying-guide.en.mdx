### Priority inspection points

- **1.6 THP (Prince EP6 engine) timing chain**: weak tensioner; carbon on intake valves.
- **THP high-pressure pump**: abnormal noise and pressure drop.
- **1.6/2.0 HDi injectors**: common failure, worsened by poor fuel.
- **DPF/FAP**: clogging on urban use; Eolys additive in a dedicated tank.
- **BSI / central locking**: intermittent reboots.

### Target price (EUR, Spanish market)

- High-mileage 1.6 HDi with history: **€3,000–5,000**.
- Well-kept 1.6 VTi petrol: **€4,500–6,500**.
- 1.6 THP (150–200 hp) in good condition: **€6,000–9,000**.
