### Priority inspection points

- **0.9 TCe (H4Bt) timing chain**: cold-start noise may indicate guide wear.
- **1.5 dCi injectors**: common failure, worsened by poor-quality fuel.
- **EDC (dry dual-clutch) gearbox**: low-speed jerkiness and operating-temp behaviour.
- **R-Link / interior electronics**: reboots, intermittent Keyless card.
- **Fuel-supply pump**: abnormal noise on diesel.

### Target price (EUR, Spanish market)

- High-mileage 1.2 petrol basic: **€3,500–5,500**.
- 1.5 dCi with history: **€5,500–8,000**.
- RS Trophy (1.6 T 220 hp, EDC): **€13,000–18,000**.
