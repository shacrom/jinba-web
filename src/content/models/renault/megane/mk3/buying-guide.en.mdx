### Priority inspection points

- **1.5 dCi injectors**: common failure — smoke, hesitation, fault codes, high consumption.
- **1.9/2.0 dCi turbo**: clogged EGR, wastegate issues.
- **Clutch and dual-mass flywheel (dCi)**: idle vibration, noises on clutch disengagement.
- **Interior electronics**: intermittent A/C, unresponsive Keyless card.
- **Underbody corrosion (Sport Tourer)**: inspect chassis and rear wheel arches.

### Target price (EUR, Spanish market)

- High-mileage 1.5 dCi with history: **€3,500–5,500**.
- Well-kept 1.6 petrol: **€5,500–7,500**.
- RS Trophy (2.0 T 265 hp): **€14,000–22,000**.
