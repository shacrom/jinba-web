### Priority inspection points

- **N47 (diesel) timing chain**: rear-located chain; endemic failure (2007–2011) — cold-start rattle = urgent.
- **Electric water pump (N52/N54/M3)**: lifespan ~80,000–120,000 km; frequent failure.
- **VANOS and Valvetronic (N43/N46)**: rough idle, hesitation.
- **N54 injectors (335i)**: piezo-injector recall; white smoke and misfires.
- **N54 HPFP**: specific recall; power loss and engine light.
- **S65 rod bearings (M3)**: wear under hard use — reinforced kit recommended.

### Target price (EUR, Spanish market)

- 318d/320d with history: **€6,000–10,000**.
- 320i/325i petrol: **€7,000–12,000**.
- Well-kept 335i (N54): **€13,000–20,000**.
- M3 E92 (S65): **€35,000–55,000** depending on mileage and condition.
