- Second-generation León.
- VAG PQ35 platform, shared with Golf Mk5/Mk6, Audi A3 8P, and Škoda Octavia Mk2.
- Petrol: 1.2/1.4 TSI, 1.8/2.0 TFSI, 2.0 TFSI Cupra R (265 hp).
- Diesel: 1.6/1.9/2.0 TDI (90–170 hp).
- Front-wheel drive; 5/6-speed manual or 6-speed wet DSG (DQ250) / 7-speed dry DSG (DQ200).
