### Priority inspection points

- **1.4 TSI (EA111) timing chain**: weak tensioner — cold-start rattle.
- **1.8/2.0 TFSI oil consumption (EA888 Gen1/2)**: pistons and rings on early engines (pre-2009).
- **7-speed dry DSG (DQ200)**: low-speed jerkiness; 6-speed wet DSG (DQ250) is sturdier.
- **DPF and EGR (TDI)**: urban-only use history = early trouble.
- **2.0 TDI CR high-pressure pump**: abnormal noise, engine code P0087.

### Target price (EUR, Spanish market)

- High-mileage 1.9 TDI with history: **€3,500–6,000**.
- Well-kept 1.4/1.8 TFSI: **€6,500–9,500**.
- Cupra R (2.0 TFSI 265 hp): **€11,000–16,000** depending on condition.
