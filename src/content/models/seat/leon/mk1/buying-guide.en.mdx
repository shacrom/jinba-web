### Priority inspection points

- **Turbocharger (K03/K04)**: shaft play, blue smoke, boost pressure.
- **Head gasket (BAM)**: endemic failure — look for mayonnaise under the oil cap and erratic levels.
- **Oil consumption**: critical on high-mileage units.
- **Front differential**: documented service history.
- **Clutch and dual-mass flywheel**: idle vibration, noises on disengagement.
