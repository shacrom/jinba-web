- Fourth-generation Ibiza (2008–2017, 6P facelift in 2012).
- VAG PQ25 platform, shared with Polo Mk5, Audi A1, and Škoda Fabia Mk2.
- Petrol: 1.2 TSI (85–110 hp), 1.4 TSI Cupra (150–180 hp).
- Diesel: 1.2/1.6/1.9/2.0 TDI (70–143 hp).
- Front-wheel drive; 5/6-speed manual or 7-speed dry DSG (DQ200).
