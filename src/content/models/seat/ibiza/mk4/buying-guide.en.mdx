### Priority inspection points

- **7-speed dry DSG (DQ200)**: low-speed jerkiness, harsh shifts — check service history (mechatronic, oil).
- **1.2 TSI (EA111) timing chain**: weak tensioner — listen for cold-start rattle.
- **Electric water pump (TSI)**: common failure, intermittent temperature warning.
- **Electric windows**: plastic regulator cracks; window sits crooked or won't raise.
- **EGR / DPF sensor (TDI)**: fault codes and power loss.

### Target price (EUR, Spanish market)

- High-mileage diesel basics: **€3,500–5,500**.
- 1.2 TSI or 1.4 TSI with clean history: **€6,500–9,000**.
- Cupra / FR with low mileage: **€10,000–14,000**.
