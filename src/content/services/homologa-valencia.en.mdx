Levante Homologaciones is an engineering firm specialised in modifications and homologation of imported vehicles across the Valencia Region. They handle individual projects and complete paperwork trails for cars made before 1990 and enthusiast imports from Japan or the United Kingdom.

They produce technical documentation, structural calculations for reinforcements, homologated engine swaps with power equivalencies, and follow the administrative process with Spanish inspection stations (ITV) and the central traffic authority.

Their approach is closer to an engineering consultancy than a workshop: they write reports, assess feasibility before any work, and coordinate with mechanical partners for the physical execution.
