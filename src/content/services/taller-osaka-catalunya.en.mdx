Osaka Garage is a small but well-established workshop in the Barcelona metro area. It focuses on Nissan Silvia S15, Toyota Supra MkIV, and three generations of MX-5, with an emphasis on builds intended for daily driving.

The shop offers three working tracks: strict maintenance (fluids, timing belts, reinforced clutches), electronic diagnostics for SR20DET and 2JZ powerplants, and mild street-oriented tuning using homologation-friendly components for occasional public trackdays.

They coordinate with local homologation specialists when required, and maintain a direct supply line with Japanese vendors to source hard-to-find parts within Spain.
