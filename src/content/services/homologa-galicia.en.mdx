Finisterre Ingeniería is an engineering office based in A Coruña with over a decade of experience homologating cars imported from Japan and the United Kingdom, serving Galicia and northern Portugal.

Their expertise covers steering conversions (RHD to LHD, or RHD kept under individual project), engine swaps with complete technical packages, and registering vehicles in the Spanish historic register.

They offer a remote pre-assessment service for clients considering an import: from photos and Japanese paperwork, they estimate feasibility before the purchase is committed.
