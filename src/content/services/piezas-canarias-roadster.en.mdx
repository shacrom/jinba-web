Atlántico Roadster Parts was founded in Las Palmas to make Japanese roadster ownership easier on the islands. Today it's a reference for MX-5, S2000, and Silvia owners across the Canary Islands and the Balearic archipelago.

Their catalog covers original and aftermarket soft-tops, top mechanisms, rear windows, door seals, and interior spares that are hard to source from mainland suppliers.

Body panels (fenders, hoods, skirts) are handled on demand through partners in Japan and Germany. Shipping to mainland Spain is subsidised for orders over €250.
