Madrid Classics has spent fifteen years maintaining Japanese classic sports cars in central Madrid. From original-spec NA and NB MX-5s to full-chassis restorations of 240Zs and Celicas.

The team combines carburetor expertise, L24 engine tuning, and preventive maintenance for cars that see daily use. They source original parts whenever possible and vet aftermarket replacements for items out of production.

They accept phone bookings and keep a steady calendar: inspections, pre-ITV checks, and tune-ups are typically reserved two weeks in advance. A small stock of common consumables (belts, thermostats, spark plugs) is kept on-site for MX-5, S30 and Hachiroku owners.
